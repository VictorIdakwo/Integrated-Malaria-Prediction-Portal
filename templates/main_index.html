This portal provides insights into malaria prediction using Machine Learning models. Choose an option below to explore predictions for clinical or non-clinical malaria cases.

Prediction Features

Clinical Malaria Prediction: Uses symptoms such as chill/cold, headache, fever, generalized body pain, abdominal pain, loss of appetite, joint pain, vomiting, nausea, and diarrhea for predictions.

Non-Clinical Malaria Prediction: Utilizes environmental factors like Rainfall, Land Surface Temperature (LST), and Relative Humidity for predictions.
